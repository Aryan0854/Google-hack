Frequently Asked Questions

Is FactCheck free to use?

Yes, FactCheck is completely free for all users. We believe reliable information should be accessible to everyone.

How does FactCheck verify information?

FactCheck uses a combination of trusted fact-checking databases, AI analysis, and journalistic standards to evaluate content.

Does FactCheck work on all websites?

FactCheck is designed to work on news websites and articles. It may not provide analysis for social media posts or personal blogs.

Does FactCheck collect my browsing data?

FactCheck only processes the content of news articles you're actively reading. We don't track your browsing history or collect personal data.
